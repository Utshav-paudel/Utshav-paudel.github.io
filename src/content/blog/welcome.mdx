There's a Jupyter notebook open on my laptop right now. There has been one open, basically, for the last four years. Notebooks are where I think. Where the half-formed idea becomes a chart, becomes a model, becomes an argument I can defend.

So when I sat down to redo my site, the question was: what would it look like if my notebook *was* my site? Not "styled to look like" one. Actually structured like one. Cells you can read top-to-bottom. A kernel status in the corner. Code that's there because the page genuinely is computed from data.

That's what this is.
